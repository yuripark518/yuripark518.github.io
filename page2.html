Interactive Dog Insight Poster
for Children

Leveraging children's exploratory learning, this poster visually showcases various dog breeds and their potential challenges based on morphological changes. It's an engaging way for kids to understand that different dogs might face different difficulties.




Project Description



Summerized Description

Re-emphasizing the reason why, recalling my own childhood misconceptions about "cute" dog breeds, I was jolted to discover the hidden pain some traits cause. Why not craft an engaging tool to enlighten kids about these truths, ensuring a more informed and compassionate future for them and our canine friends?

Building on my prior research about educating pre-schoolers, as detailed in the "topic knowing" section, the selected method of conveying information was designed to be child-friendly and infographic-based.
